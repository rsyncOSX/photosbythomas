My name is Thomas, [thomeven@gmail.com](mailto:thomeven@gmail.com). I retired from work in April 2022 at the age of 62 and I am a passionate photographer of wildlife (most of birds) and nature. Grand kids, photography, continue maintenance of my macOS applications [RsyncOSX](https://github.com/rsyncOSX/RsyncOSX) and [RsyncUI](https://github.com/rsyncOSX/RsyncUI) and beeing in the Norwegian mountains keeps me busy.

The work on this website commenced July 2022 and will be enhanced and expanded during 2022.

If there are photos you want to buy for print or other purposes please contact me by email. All photos on this web are taken by me.

## Why this web

These webpages are for two purposes only: 

- present some of my work with the camera

- educate myself about birds I am taking pictures of

Facts and figures about what is photographed are linked to within the pages. I have also to say I am no expert whatsoever about birds.

If there are photos you want to buy for print or other purposes please contact me by email. All photos on this web are taken by me.

For the last 5 years I have only used Sony gear. Previous Sony camera bodies are Sony fullframes A7iii and Sony A9ii. I have also used lenses from Tamron and Sigma, but all are sold.

My gear now are Sony fullframe A1 camera body and Sony FE 400mm F2.8 GM OSS lens with Sony FE 1.4x teleconverter. The 400mm lens is primarly bought because it is a superb lens for bird photography. The 1.4TC extend the 400mm to 560mm at F4.0 (two stops down).
