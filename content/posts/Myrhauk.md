Circus cyaneus or [Hen harrier - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hen_harrier)   photographed in the mountains close to Ål, Hallingdal (Norway) July 2022. The male and female has different feathers.  The female is brown and white. The male is light grey with black wingtips.

By click on a photo you can enlarge and browse through all photos in this page by using the browser.

The female and male is a couple. When I photographed the couple they warned me and wanted me to leave the area. I stayed in the area for a short time only before I left.

In Norwegian: [Myrhauk – Wikipedia](https://no.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myrhauk).
