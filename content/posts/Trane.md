Grus grus or [Common crane - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Common_crane)   photographed at Ål in Hallingdal, Norway. The crane couple is nesting close to our cabin. For the last three years the cranes has got two young ones and I am very happy to observe that both kids makes it through this summer as well.

In Norwegian: [Trane – Wikipedia](https://no.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trane).
