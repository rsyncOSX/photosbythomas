Northern hawk-owl, Short-eared owl, Tawny owl and Eurasian pygmy owl. The three last owls I have photographed close
to where I live. The Northern hawk-owl, which is quite common owl, is photographed in the mountains. Only the Tawny owl
of these four owls, are hunting by night. All three others are hunting in daylight.
