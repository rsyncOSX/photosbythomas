Buteo buteo or [Common buzzard - Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Common_buzzard)  photographed close to Horten, Norway August 2022.  The picture of the buzzard with a mouse, I presume it is a mouse, was taken a few weeks earlier. I discoverd the buzzard when driving on the highway. I drove of the highway as soon as I could and luckily the buzzard was still there when I safely parked the car on a sideway of the highway.
